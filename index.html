EvolveCyber's mission is to teach IT and transform the lives of people of all backgrounds. Our 5-month DevOps course is designed specifically for beginners. It is part-time and one can attend it either online or onsite. Our curriculum was derived from most in demand DevOps skills. Take advantage of our adapted and perfected curriculum in an environment designed for your success. We will teach you how to use automation techniques through real-life scenarios, practical labs and projects. Each cohort is part time and lasts for 23 weeks. It covers Automation, CI/CD, Networking, Security, Containerization, Virtualization, Cloud, Scripting, IaaC, and more.
